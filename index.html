EU Commission Transparency Analysis

Public Consultation Data Sharing Practices 2017–2025
657 Consultations Analysed

Key Finding

Transparency peaked in 2020 and has declined every year since. Full data sharing dropped from 72.6% to 15.1%, while consultations with no published data rose from 17.9% to 47.9%.
